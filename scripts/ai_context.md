## ‚úÖ Eligibility Criteria (Who can donate?)
- **Age**: 18 to 65 years old.
- **Weight**: At least 50 kg (for whole blood) / 45 kg (minimal, but 50+ preferred).
- **Hemoglobin**: Minimum 12.5 g/dL.
- **Health**: Must be in good general health, feeling well on the day of donation.
- **Pulse**: Between 50 and 100 beats per minute.
- **Blood Pressure**: Systolic 100-180 mmHg, Diastolic 50-100 mmHg.
- **Temperature**: Normal oral temperature not exceeding 37.5¬∞C (99.5¬∞F).

## ‚è≥ Donation Intervals
- **Whole Blood**: Every 3 months for men, every 4 months for women.
- **Platelets (Apheresis)**: Can be donated every 7 days (up to 24 times a year).
- **Plasma**: Every 28 days.

## üö´ Common Deferrals (When to wait?)
- **Alcohol**: Wait 24 hours after consuming alcohol.
- **Antibiotics**: Wait 3 days after finishing the course.
- **Tattoo/Piercing**: Wait 12 months (due to risk of infections like Hepatitis).
- **Surgery**: Wait 6-12 months depending on the procedure (consult a doctor).
- **Pregnancy/Breastfeeding**: Wait 12 months after delivery; avoid donating while breastfeeding.
- **Fever/Cold**: Wait until fully recovered and symptom-free for at least 7 days.
- **Dental Work**: Wait 24 hours for minor work (cleaning/filling), 3 days for extractions.

### How to Find a Hospital/Blood Bank?
1.  Log in to the HemoHive app.
2.  Go to the "Find Blood" or "Hospitals" section.
3.  Allow location access or search by City/Pincode.
4.  You will see a list of nearby hospitals with available blood stock.

### How to Request Blood?
1.  Go to the "Requests" tab.
2.  Click "New Request".
3.  Select Patient Name, Blood Group, Hospital, and Urgency.
4.  Upload the Doctor's Prescription (mandatory).
5.  Submit. Nearby donors will be notified immediately.

### How "Credits" Work
- **Earning**: You earn **1 Credit** per verified blood donation.
- **Using**: Credits can be used to request blood priority or waive processing charges at partner hospitals (feature in beta).
- **Transfer**: You can transfer credits to family members in emergencies via the "Wallet" section.
